Welcome to Green UV

The UV-C Light Of The Green UV Systems Keeps Mold, Bacteria And Other Bio-Contamination From Growing On The Moist Areas Of HVAC Evaporator Coils. Keeping Your HVAC Evaporator Coil Clean Reduce Mold Spores And Allergen Counts And Prevents Bio Growth From Clogging Up Your HVAC Evaporator Coil Fins And Drain Line. Clogging Fins Insulate And Restrict Air Flow Causing The HVAC System To Work Harder And Wasting Significant Energy.

About us

About us

At Green UV, we take indoor air quality very seriously. We’ve designed and developed innovative solutions to persistent threats to your health, including mold, bacteria, viruses and allergens. All of our products use less energy than our competitors’, and work to remove bacterial agents from indoor air by up to 99.9%.

The Highest Quality Standards

We believe that delivering the highest quality indoor air quality solutions to you, our customer, is to start with the right research and development (R&D) steps. We engineer our products to the highest quality from the very beginning, with rigorous quality assurance and quality testing along the way. This ensures that each UV light system is manufactured and shipped without defects, completely tested for operability and quality.
